Meet Zachary

I grew up in Galway, and went to school at Galway CSD. Right in the middle of our district. As I was growing up, I joined Cub Scouts and later ascended into the Boy Scouts of America. My time as a member of these organizations instilled in me many values and lessons that I still carry with me to this day, including: Leadership, Fellowship, and a deep connection with our environment and community.

Why I'm Running

In these decisive political times, I think it is more important than ever that we treat all our neighbors with respect and compassion. We need someone who cares about our communities and our environment. Who cares about people, who through no fault of their own, are unable to afford housing, or food, or medical care, or anything else necessary for the pursuit of life, liberty, and happiness.

 

I hope to be the person you choose to make these dreams a reality.
