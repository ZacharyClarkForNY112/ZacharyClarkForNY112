HOW TO SUBMIT A WRITE-IN BALLOT

This is a sample ballot from a past election in Oswego County. Your ballot on November 6th WILL look different.

To cast a vote for me, fill out your ballot as normal except for the column labeled "MEMBER OF ASSEMBLY 112th District". Do not fill out any bubbles in this column. Instead, go to the bottom row of the column, in the box labeled "WRITE-IN", and write my name, Zachary Clark, as clearly as possible, then submit your ballot.
